GEMVAS Scheme (Government Employees Motor Vehicle Advance Scheme)

Eligibility:

Available to all permanent and pensionable civil servants who have completed their probation period.

How to Apply:
  1. Download the GEMVAS Application Form.
  2. Obtain a pro-forma invoice from a recognized motor vehicle dealer.
  3. Submit the completed form to the Ministry of Finance GEMVAS desk.
Required Documents:
  • Certified copy of National ID (Omang)
  • Latest three months' payslips
  • Valid Driver's License
  • Letter of confirmation of employment
